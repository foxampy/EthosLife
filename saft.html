2. Definitions

"TGE" (Token Generation Event) means the date on which UNITY utility tokens are minted and deployed on the Ethereum blockchain (or Layer 2 solution), estimated to occur within 90 days of the date hereof, but in no event later than 180 days from the date of this Agreement.

"UNITY" means the ERC-20 utility token issued by the Company, providing access to the EthosLife Human Operating System platform, governance participation rights in the EthosLife DAO (upon launch), and eligibility for platform staking mechanisms.

"Staking Boost Voucher" means a non-transferable digital right granted to early investors as compensation for the waiting period prior to TGE, entitling the holder to an enhanced Annual Percentage Yield (APY) of 25% (instead of the standard 15% target APY) during the first 6 months following the completion of the Cliff Period.

"Jurisdiction Election" means the final selection of the Company's incorporation jurisdiction, which shall be determined by the Founder within 30 days of the first investment closing, selected from: Cayman Islands, Republic of Cyprus, UAE (RAK DAO or similar blockchain-friendly zone), or another jurisdiction mutually acceptable to the Parties.

"Vesting Commencement Date" means the TGE.

"Cliff Period" means 6 months from the Vesting Commencement Date.

"Vesting Period" means 18 months following the Cliff Period (24 months total from TGE).

  • Lifetime Premium Access: Immediate access to EthosLife Premium tier features upon platform launch (prior to token issuance), valid for the lifetime of the Investor's account.
  • Price Lock: Guaranteed allocation at $0.05 per token, regardless of future pricing in subsequent rounds.
